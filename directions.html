Parking

Where to Park

On-Site Lot

A free parking lot sits directly adjacent to the venue. It's a great option if you arrive early — spaces fill quickly once the evening gets going, so plan accordingly for a large event.

Street Parking

Side streets off Mystic Ave have free street parking on evenings and Sundays. Always check posted signs for time limits and resident permit zones. Medford meters are enforced Mon–Sat, 8:30 AM–6:00 PM.

Our recommendation: For a wedding-sized event, the on-site lot may fill up. We encourage guests to rideshare or take public transit when possible — it makes for a better evening for everyone.

Driving

Directions by Car

N

From the North

▾

Via I-93 South from Woburn, Stoneham, Reading, or I-95

  1. Take I-93 South toward Boston.
  2. Take Exit 32 (Route 60 / Salem St — Medford Square / Malden).
  3. Bear left off the ramp and follow Route 60 briefly toward Medford Square.
  4. Turn onto Mystic Ave (Route 38) heading south.
  5. 142 Mystic Ave will be on your right.

Alternate: Exit 30 (Route 38 / Mystic Ave — Somerville) and head north on Mystic Ave.

S

From the South & Boston

▾

Via I-93 North from downtown Boston or Logan Airport

  1. Take I-93 North from Boston toward Somerville / Medford.
  2. Take Exit 22 (Route 16 West — Mystic Valley Pkwy).
  3. Follow Route 16 West briefly from the ramp.
  4. Turn right (north) onto Mystic Ave (Route 38).
  5. 142 Mystic Ave will be on your left shortly after.

From Logan: Follow signs to the Sumner Tunnel, then I-93 North and use the directions above.

W

From the West

▾

Via Route 2 East from Lexington, Concord, or western suburbs

  1. Take Route 2 East past I-95 toward Cambridge / Arlington.
  2. At the junction of Routes 2 and 16, bear left onto Route 16 East (Mystic Valley Pkwy).
  3. Follow Route 16 East as it curves north along the Mystic Valley Parkway.
  4. Turn right (south) onto Mystic Ave (Route 38).
  5. 142 Mystic Ave will be on your left.

GPS search for "The Great American Beer Hall, Medford, MA" works reliably.

Public Transit

Getting Here by MBTA

BUS
95

Bus Route 95 — Most Direct

The 95 runs directly along Mystic Ave and stops at Mystic Ave @ Hancock St — right out front of the venue. Board the 95 at Sullivan Square Station (Orange Line) heading north toward Medford.

Stop: Mystic Ave @ Hancock St
GL
E

Green Line — E Branch

Take the Green Line E branch to the Medford/Tufts terminus. The venue is roughly a 10-minute walk (~0.7 miles) southeast along Mystic Ave.

Station: Medford/Tufts · ~10 min walk
OL

Orange Line + Bus 95

Take the Orange Line to Sullivan Square Station, then board Bus 95 northbound. Ride it up Mystic Ave and exit at the Hancock St stop directly in front of the venue.

Transfer: Sullivan Square → Bus 95 North

Prefer Uber or Lyft? The venue is easy to find — just enter
